Planning Tips

  1. Start your day by writing your top priorities.
  2. Keep your task list realistic so it feels manageable.
  3. Break big assignments into smaller steps.
  4. Use breaks to avoid getting burnt out.
  5. Give yourself deadlines to stay on track.
  6. Review what you finished at the end of the day and feel accomplished of your hard work!
