Project Purpose

Bloom is a daily planner designed to feel approachable, motivating, and stress free! The goal of this project is to help users organize their day while feeling relaxed and encouraged.

Bloom lets you add your daily tasks to the checklist, helping you organize your day. The progress bar allows you to visualize your productivity each time a task is checked.

Bloom also includes a daily fun fact and word of the day to improve your knowledge. The built in focus timer allows you to stay on task while completing your daily to-dos.

Key Features

  • Custom to do list
  • Progress updates
  • Daily fun fact
  • Word of the day
  • Focus timer
